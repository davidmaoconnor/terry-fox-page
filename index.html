Terry Fox
The Man Who Ran Across Canada for the Cure

Timeline

Date Event
7/28/1958 Terry Fox is born in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada.
3/08/1977 Terry reads about an amputee runner while in the hospital for his leg.
3/09/1977 Terry's right leg is amputated. He has bone cancer.
2/1979 Terry decides to run the enitre length of Canada to raise awareness and money for cancer reseach. He also begins training.
4/12/1980 Terry begins his run by dipping his artificial leg into the Atlantic Ocean. He intends to finish by dipping the same leg into the Pacific Ocean.
9/01/1980 Terry has run for 143 days and 5,373 kilmetres. His cancer has spread to his lungs and he has to stop running.
2/01/1981 Through Terry's efforts, $1 is raised for every Canadian in the country: Over 24 million dollars.
6/28/1981 Terry dies at Royal Columbian Hospital in British Columbia, Canada.
